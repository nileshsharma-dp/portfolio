    About Nilesh Sharma

    I have a background in Biotechnology, and my journey into Data Science and Machine Learning is a fascinating transition. My experience in biotech has honed my analytical skills, and data science provides the perfect avenue to extend my passion for problem-solving and innovation.

    What excites me most about this field is its ever-evolving nature, the dynamic challenges it presents, and the continuous learning it requires. I strongly believe in the power of data to revolutionize industries and drive impactful decisions, and I am eager to contribute to this transformation.
